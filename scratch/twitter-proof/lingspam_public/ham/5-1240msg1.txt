Subject: internet - accessible linguistic data-sources

subscribers have asked us to compile a list of linguistic resources which are available on the internet and on world wide web , and to make that list generally available through the linguist file and web servers . we ' ve also been asked to compile a list of linguistic departments and programs which have either web servers , or information accessible through gopher or ftp . we ' re happy to do this - - we think it would be very useful resource for subscribers - - but we need you to help us , by providing the information that file will contain . these resources can be of any nature : they can be ftp ' able files containing linguistic data , places where gopher servers having access to linguistic files exist , or documents available through web servers . we ' ve appended the list of linguistic resources we have so far . we know this is incomplete . can you help us find what 's missing - - and keep us informed of anything new that appears as time passes ? regards anthony & helen _
